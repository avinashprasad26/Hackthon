Elderly adults are classified between the ages of 65 and 74 years as youngest-old, those between ages 75 and 84 years as middle-old, and those aged over 85 years as oldest-old. Few don't know or tend to forget how to handle a computer device or even whats inside a computer. This section is ment for helping the elderly group to learn about computer parts, openining and closing files and applications, basics of internet surfing and e-mail usage, and a bit about creating and saving documents.
